Babyshuo.com

2011.11 - 2013.02

Description:

•Babyshuo.com is a place for young moms to share and obtain their favorite fashion clothes to dress up their kids

•With a crawler built for Taobao.com (biggest C2C e-commerce with over a billion products on sale), users can easily fetch the information (including price, description, pictures etc.) of the clothes they like and post it on Babyshuo.com

•Users can follow other users, like or collect others' posts. If they want to buy the clothes they saw, simply click on a button and it will bring them back to Taobao.com to finish their purchases

Business Model:

•For every clothes sold with the help of Babyshuo.com, the website gained revenue with the repayments from stores on Taobao.com
